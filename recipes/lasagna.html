Description

This homemade lasagna is the ultimate comfort food layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic, onion, basil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the meat with onions and garlic
  3. Layer noodles, meat, sauce, and cheese
  4. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes
  5. Let cool and enjoy!
