Description

Classic ground beef tacos with crunchy shells, savory spices, and fresh toppings. A weeknight favorite that's quick and tasty.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Taco shells
  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Brown the beef in a skillet
  2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Warm the taco shells
  5. Fill with meat and toppings
  6. Serve and enjoy!
