Description

Pad Thai is a stir-fried noodle dish from Thailand made with rice noodles, a sweet-savory sauce, vegetables, and your choice of protein. Itâ€™s tangy, nutty, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Chicken or shrimp
  • Eggs
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions
  • Pad Thai sauce (tamarind, fish sauce, sugar)
  • Crushed peanuts
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water
  2. Cook protein in a wok or pan
  3. Add eggs and scramble
  4. Add noodles and Pad Thai sauce
  5. Toss in bean sprouts and green onions
  6. Garnish with peanuts and lime
